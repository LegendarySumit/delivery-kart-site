Frequently Asked Questions

How do I track my delivery?

Use your tracking ID in the “Track Order” section on our homepage. You’ll see live location, expected delivery time, and contact options.

How can I request a refund?

If your order was canceled or delayed beyond our SLA, visit “Payments & Refunds” to raise a claim. Refunds are processed within 5 business days.

Is my data safe with Delivery Kart?

Yes. We are fully GDPR and ISO 27001 compliant. All user data is encrypted end-to-end for your safety.

How do I contact live chat support?

Click “Contact Support” or use the chat bubble in the bottom-right corner of any page to reach our team instantly.

Still Need Help?

Our customer success team is available 24/7 for any delivery, payment, or account-related issues.
